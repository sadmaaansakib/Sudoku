AI Lab Insight

Constraint Satisfaction Problem

Sudoku is modeled as a CSP where cells are variables, domains are numbers [1-9], and rules form binary constraints.

MRV (Minimum Remaining Values) Heuristic

Instead of iterating row-by-row, MRV selects the cell with the fewest possible valid candidates. This prunes invalid branches early and reduces search time dramatically.
